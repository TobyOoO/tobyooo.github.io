本作品「台孩兒養成器」由台孩製作委員會（TaiKids Production Comittee）製作。
本故事內容為聯經出版 2026 小說《台孩危機》衍生作品，原版權歸原著及出版方所有。
本作品採 創用條款 4.0 姓名標示-相同分享方式 授權；程式原始碼多由 Gemini 3.0 Preview 產生。

This work, "TaiKids Simulator", is produced by TaiKids Production Comittee.
The story is a derivative from the novel "Crisis of Kids at the Strait" (台孩危機) published by Linking Publish.
This work is licensed under CC BY-SA 4.0.
Most of source code in this work are generated by Gemini 3.0 Preview.
